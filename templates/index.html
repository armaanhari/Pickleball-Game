⚡ Power-ups Guide

BIG
Big Paddle Your paddle grows to 1.6× its normal height for 5 seconds.
SPD
Speed Burst Your next hit launches the ball at maximum speed, regardless of hand movement.
SHR
Shrink Your opponent's paddle shrinks to half its normal height for 4 seconds.
Power-ups appear on the court every ~7 seconds. The ball collects them — whichever player last hit the ball gets the effect.
